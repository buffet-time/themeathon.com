Guidelines for our 3rd party events.

What we offer!

  • An already existing community that is tolerant to various games and events.
  • help in organizing your events to make the most of them!
  • Experienced Staff and Moderation team!
  • Experience interacting with Twitch staff for possible front page promotion. (This requires many months in advance notice)

Our requirements

Of course we have strict requirements when it comes to 3rd party events. But if you have a really good and/or unique idea we will lower them a bit.

Requirements:
  • Send us a detailed plan of your event
  • What game/ games will there be?
  • How long will the event last?
  • Will the event have a prize pool? (How much or what will be given away)
  • Are you providing a Team? (GFX, Streamers, Commentators, Moderators)
  • Are you experienced with organizing events?
  • Tell something about yourself?
  • Your expectations?

After you have thought about everything and understand the requirements/ guidelines, feel free to submit your idea to us!

Where and how can I submit my event?

What timeslot will my event be in?

We try to find the best possible timeslot for your event. But keep in mind that we are just a small team and we can't interfere with already planned events or our main events. After we find a possible time window we will get in contact with you and your team.
