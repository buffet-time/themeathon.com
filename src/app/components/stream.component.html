HEMEATHON

Themeathon is a speedrunning marathon for charity founded by Lordmau5 and Jugachi in 2016.

Each of our marathons raises money for the Save the Children Federation and has a specific theme that guides the content provided to you by our lovely hosts. Thus far we've raised over $10,000 for Save the Children, and we plan on raising much much more!

The official marathon this year will be Japan themed!
If you have a run, feel welcome to submit for the official marathon! Don't worry about conforming to the Japan theme, it's not required for any runs.

THEMEATHON will happen in late autumn of this year! Stay tuned for specific dates and location!
